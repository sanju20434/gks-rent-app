# Project Report - GKS Rent Pro

## 1. Problem Statement

Construction rental businesses need a simple, integrated workflow for inventory, client billing, payment tracking, and communication.

## 2. Objectives

- Manage clients, projects, and items
- Track material movement and invoices
- Track outstanding dues and payments
- Improve communication via WhatsApp and QR payment flows

Refer to `architecture.md` and `erd.md`.

## 4. Features Implemented

- Client management
- Item master and stock status
- Booking, material OUT/IN, billing
- Payment and deposit tracking
- Ledger and dashboard analytics
- WhatsApp due and custom messaging
- QR generation and WhatsApp QR sharing
